How it Works

Triage in three steps

From patient arrival to documented assessment in under two minutes.

1

Patient Speaks

Patient describes symptoms in any language. Whisper captures and transcribes in real-time with automatic language detection.

2

AI Analyzes

Claude extracts clinical features and generates reasoning. LightGBM validates the ESI score against trained clinical data.

3

Nurse Confirms

Triage nurse reviews the AI recommendation, confirms or adjusts, and the assessment auto-syncs to the EHR.
